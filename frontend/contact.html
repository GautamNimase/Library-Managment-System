Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Register" button in the top navigation, fill out the registration form with your details, and verify your email address. It's completely free!

How many books can I borrow at once?

Regular users can borrow up to 5 books at a time, while premium members can borrow up to 10 books. Each book can be borrowed for 14 days with the option to renew.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes! Our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play Store.

How do I report a technical issue?

You can report technical issues through our contact form by selecting "Bug Report" as the subject, or email us directly at support@librarypro.com.

Can I suggest new books to be added?

Absolutely! We love hearing book suggestions from our users. Use the contact form and select "Feature Request" as the subject to suggest new books.
